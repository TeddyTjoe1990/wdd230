Site Name

This site is going to be called Batavia Commerce with the domain BataviaCommerce.com. As the name indicates, it will be a website containing knowledge regarding the chains of businesses in the city of Jakarta, Indonesia.

Site Purpose

The main purpose of the BataviaCommerce.com site will be to promote more business owners to join the community by emphasizing the benefits and purposes of chamber of commerce groups. This objective will be attained by providing information about the town whether it be about community information, weather, business spotlights, historical information, and much more. The site will have a “join” page and a “contact” page wherein potential members will be able to try out and communicate with the organization.
