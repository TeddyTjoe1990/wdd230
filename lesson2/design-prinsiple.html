Design Principles Document

Teddy Tjoe

Alignment

churchofjesuschrist.org

This design principle is demonstrated here because the elements are aligned properly with each other on the page. Items are center aligned, and the text is also centered, which compliments the layout of the page.

Proximity

scriptures.byu.edu

This page demonstrates proximity by showing items close to each other that are related such as the scriptures and their associated references to general conference talks and other gospel topics.

Repetition

youtube.com

This example shows repetition because each element within the YouTube page is repeated over and over again, with varying titles, images and channels. The layout of the channel name, the image, and the title remains the same and is repeated.
