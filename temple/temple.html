Temple History

History

October 1 2011 - Announced

May 12 2012 - Groundbreaking

March 20 2016 - Dedicated

Summary

Plans to construct the temple were announced 1 October 2011, and Church leaders broke ground 12 May 2012. The Provo City Center Temple will be the 16th temple built in Utah, the fourth in Utah County and the second in Provo, following the Provo Utah Temple (1972). When dedicated the Provo City Center Temple will be the 150th operating temple worldwide. It will stand on the site of the former Provo Tabernacle, which was tragically lost to fire.

The temple comprises 85,084 square feet and is 150 feet high.
